About

Welcome to my website! I am Dr. Alberto Carraro, a researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence for Precision Agriculture at TESAF Department of the University of Padua (Italy). With a passion for technology and agriculture, I strive to develop innovative AI solutions to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of agricultural practices.

My expertise includes machine learning, computer vision, data analysis, and robotics. I am committed to bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and the agricultural sector to address various challenges faced by farmers and improve crop productivity.

Research

In my research, I currently focus on applying AI techniques to solve key challenges in precision agriculture. In the past I conducted research in the field of Theoretical Computer Science. Some of the areas I am currently exploring include:

  • Automated crop monitoring and yield prediction
  • Intelligent pest and disease detection
  • AI-driven irrigation and resource management
  • Robotic agriculture and autonomous farming
