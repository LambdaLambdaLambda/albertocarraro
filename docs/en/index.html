    Research and Professional Interests

    My research and teaching experience spans from the theoretical foundations of computer science to applied artificial intelligence. I specialize in:

    • Teaching and Training: Training computer science students and professionals in theoretical and applied aspects of AI, programming, and systems design.
    • Computational Formalisms: Lambda calculus and functional programming paradigms, semantic and syntactic analysis of formal systems
    • Concurrent Systems: Model-checking, Petri networks, and formalization of system properties
    • Deep Learning & Computer Vision: Practical applications of neural networks for real-world problems
    • Precision Agriculture & Food Quality: AI-driven solutions for crop disease diagnosis, livestock farming optimization, and food waste reduction

    Current Position

    Since September 2017, I am a permanent teacher in the Italian public school system, working under the Italian Ministry of Education and Merit (MIM).

    Areas of Expertise

    Theoretical Foundations: Formal calculi, lambda calculus (pure, typed, resource-based, differential), theoretical computer science, and mathematical semantics.

    Practical Applications: Computer vision using deep learning techniques, precision agriculture diagnostics, food quality control systems, and digital transformation assessment.

    Teaching: University-level and high school-level instruction in mathematics, computer science, and specialized topics in formal methods and applied AI.
